Understanding Pitta

The Pitta dosha governs metabolism, digestion, and transformation in the body. Individuals with a dominant Pitta constitution are known for their sharp intellect, ambition, and strong digestion. When in balance, Pitta individuals are warm, intelligent, and good leaders. When out of balance, they can be prone to irritability, inflammation, and acidity.

Common Characteristics of Pitta:
  • Strong appetite and digestion
  • Focused and determined mindset
  • Sharp, penetrating intelligence
  • Assertive and confident nature
  • Tendency towards perfectionism
  • Dislikes heat and hot weather
  • Natural leadership qualities
  • Prone to skin sensitivity & inflammation
